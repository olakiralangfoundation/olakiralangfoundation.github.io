About Us

Olakira Lang Foundation is a grassroots non-profit based in Siha District, Kilimanjaro, Tanzania. Our mission is to empower Maasai girls through education, mentorship, and advocacy, protecting them from early marriage and harmful cultural practices.

Our Programs

  • School Materials & Scholarships
  • Mentorship for Maasai Girls
  • Community Awareness Campaigns
  • Leadership & Life Skills Training
